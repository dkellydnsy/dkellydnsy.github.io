  • There are many examples of "Threes" throughout time (human history) and space (worldwide across geographies and cultures).
    So many things are represented and recognized utilizing threes to convey information (and possibly to impart wisdom, too).

  • I find it particularly interesting that this class project is dependent heavily on Threes (3's).
    There are 3 colors in the RGB (Red-Blue-Green) Color model which forms the basis of the CSS HEX CODES used in the website.
    The 6 Hexadecimal numbers are 3 pairs of colors representing a two digit hexadeximal value for Red 1st, then Green 2nd, & finally Blue 3rd.
    The 1st Hex Code: #000000 represents BLACK (which is the absence/absorbtion of ALL colors)...
    ... and the last (final possible color) Hex Code: #FFFFFF represents White (which is the reflection of ALL colors)...
